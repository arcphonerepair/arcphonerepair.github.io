At Arc Phone Repair your happiness is our mission. We understand going to the store, dropping your phone off and waiting to pick it back up is time consuming and a burden. That’s why we operate on a “we bring the service to you” mentality.

How Does It Work?

Give Us A Call!

Shoot us a call, text or email and we'll diagnose your phone as well as set up a time to come to your work or home to get you back up and running!

Fix Your Device

Home, Work or anywhere you need your repair done let us know! We'll bring our own van and tools to repair your phone on the spot.

After It's All Done

Like our service? If you do feel free to leave us a review it helps us serve other great customers like you.
